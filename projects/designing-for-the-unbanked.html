DESIGNING FOR THE UNBANKED

Background

Zingr is a fintech start up building a digital payments for the unbanked and underbanked in developing markets around the world using blockchain technology.

the problem

The problem of the lack of digital banking solutions was identified by Zingr Founder & CEO Giselle Frederick, shortly after the start up was formed we planned to translate the initial idea and solution into an elegant prototype.

users

The app and website was targeted to investors and early adopters of the product.

MY ROLE

I collaborated remotely with the founder on this project. My role includedVisual Design of initial prototype using Sketch, In-vision & MarvelBuilding the website to accompany the prototype.

THE PROCESS

The first thing we did was come up with sketches so we could solidify what the final design would look like. I also conducted rapid market research to see what the standards were for a fin-tech app in this particular space. Once the sketches were complete I used Sketch to come up with the designs
